Introduction


From chapters 25 to 52 of Jeremiah, the Septuagint differs in the order of the contents. For instance if you were studying Jeremiah 50:6 in an English, Hebrew, or Vulgate Bible and wanted to see the equivalent verse in the LXX, you would have to look in Jeremiah 27:6. This difficulty can be frustrating for those who study the Scriptures. Therefore, I have added a re-arranged text of Jeremiah to correspond with the English, Hebrew, Vulgate order. Now, when you look for Jeremiah 50:6, you will find it in Jeremiah (English Order) 50:6.
