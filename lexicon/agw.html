ἄγω
  • Meaning:
    • properly: to lead
    • by implication:
      • to bring
      • to drive
      • to cause (someone) to move
    • reflexively:
      • to go
      • pass (time)
    • figuratively: to induce
    • to organize and hold (a formal, public event)
    • to treat
    • to rear (a child) as a parent
    • to maintain (a particular attitude)
    • to take (someone) as a bride
