ἐκχέω
  • Active Meaning:
    • to pour out
    • to shed (blood) (i.e., commit murder)
    • to spill out
    • to bring forth
    • to pour out soil to make a mound
    • to send out in large numbers
    • to lose balance
    • to pull out
  • Passive Meaning:
    • to come forth
    • to be poured out, poured forth, rushed forth
    • to give up, abandon oneself
