  • Meaning:
    • Preposition:
      • below, underneath, beneath, bottom, down, under
    • Adverb:
      • downward
      • relatively late in history
      • down below
    • Adjective:
      • situated very low
      • Comparative: lower, inferior (locally, of Hades)
      • Superlative: lowest
