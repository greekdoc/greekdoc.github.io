ἀπόλλυμι, ἀπόλλω, ἀπολλύω
  • Active Meaning:
    • to ruin, destroy
    • to demolish, dismantle, trash, wreck
    • to lose
    • to take away
    • to erase, blot out
    • to conceal and make inaccessible
    • to be unavailable, be taken away
    • not to be found, have vanished, have gone missing
  • Middle Meaning:
    • to be destroyed, ruined
    • to perish, die
    • to be lost
