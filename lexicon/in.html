ἵνα
  • Parse: Conjunction
  • Meaning:
    • in order that (indicating purpose)
    • so that (indicating result)
    • that (used to introduce an object clause)
    • +negative = lest, in case

      ἵνα μὴ ἀποθάνητε
      lest you die (Gen 3:3)

    • why?

      ἵνα τί
      for why...?,
      for what reason or purpose ...?,
      for what good ...?       (Gen 4:6; 25:32)

  • Forms:
    • ἵν᾿ Conjunction
