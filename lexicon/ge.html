γέ, γε
  • Parse: particle
  • Meaning:
    • and besides, doubtless, at least, yet
  • Combination:
    • ἀλλά γε = at least
    • ἄρα γε = therefore indeed
    • γέ τοι = indeed
    • εἴ γε = if indeed, inasmuch as (2Cor 5:3; Col 1:23)
    • εἰ δὲ μήγε (εἰ δὲ μή γε) = otherwise (Matt 9:17; Luke 5:37; 10:6; 13:9; Matt 9:17; 2Cor 11:16)
    • καί γε = at least, even
    • καίτοι γε = and yet; though of course
    • μενοῦν γε = rather
    • μήτι γε = not to mention, let alone
    • ὄφελόν γε = would that indeed
    • πέρας γέ τοι = furthermore
