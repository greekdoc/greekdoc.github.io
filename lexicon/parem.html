  • Active Meaning:
    • to set up camp, encamp, camp, pitch camp
    • to take up temporary quarters

    • to gather together, muster (troops)

    • to insinuate oneself (in anger)
    • to put in beside, insert, interpolate, interpose
    • to interpose oneself
