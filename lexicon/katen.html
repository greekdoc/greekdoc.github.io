  • Parse: Adverb
  • Meaning:
    • before, in the presence of
    • opposite to
    • over against; directly opposite
    • in opposition to, faced with
    • facing, in front of

      ὑπάγετε εἰς τὴν κατέναντι κώμην
      Go into the village in front of you(Luke 19:30)
