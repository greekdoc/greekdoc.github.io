  • Parse: Adverb
  • Meaning:
    • Of Place: from here
    • Of Time: from then
    • Of Position: on this side (from speaker's perspective)

      ἔνθεν καὶ ἔνθεν ἦσαν γεγραμμέναι
      they were written on this side and on that side (Ex 32:15)
