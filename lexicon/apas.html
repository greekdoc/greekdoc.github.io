ἅπας
  • Parse: Adj: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning:
    • whole
    • all (things)
    • every (one)
    • the universe

    οὐ ἅπας
    none, not any

    ἅπας + article + noun
    all (e.g., all the city)

    article + ἅπας + noun
    noun = whole (e.g., the whole city)

    (no article) + ἅπας + noun
    every, any (e.g., every city, any city)
