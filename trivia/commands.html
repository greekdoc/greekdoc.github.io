Ten Commandments

Commands

Is there a correct order of the Ten Commandments? Certainly the first group regarding the one true God, forbidding idolatry, taking God's name in vain, and rembering the Sabbath seem to be consistent.

But what about the rest? While the order in the Septuagint differs from the Hebrew text, why does Jesus recite them in a different order. Or more precisely why do the Gospel writers record them in a different order. Even Paul, trained as a Pharisee, does not follow the Hebrew or the Septuagint. Is the order important?

  • MT=honour parents, murder, adultery, stealing, telling truth, coveting
  • LXX=honour parents, adultery, stealing, murder, telling truth, coveting
  • Matt 19:18ff = murder, adultery, stealing, telling truth, honour parents, loving neighbour
  • Mark 10:19 = adultery, murder, stealing, telling truth, defrauding, honour parents
  • Luke 18:20 = adultery, murder, stealing, telling truth, honour parents
  • Rom 13:9 = adultery, murder, stealing, telling truth, coveting
