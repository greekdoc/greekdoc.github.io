Shortest Verse

What is the shortest verse in the New Testament? Many people would respond, "Jesus wept" (John 11:35). It is true in the English Bibles; but not in Greek.

εἰ δέ τις ἀγνοεῖ, ἀγνοεῖται
But if anyone is ignorant, let him be ignorant (1Cor 14:38)

22 letters, 5 words

pray without ceasing (1Thess 5:17)

21 letters, 5 words

Some people draw great conclusions from the fact that "Jesus wept" is the shortest verse because it shows the compassion of the Lord. However, since the inspired Scripture is not the English translation, but the Greek; the shortest verse really doesn't have such significance. Luke 20:30 is found in the account that the Sadducees brought to Jesus to prove that there is no physical resurrection. Seven brothers in succession had married the one woman. "Whose wife will she be in the resurrection?"
