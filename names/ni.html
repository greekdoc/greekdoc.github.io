Νίγερ
  • Meaning: Niger
  • Note: Surname of Simeon (Acts 13:1)
Νικάνωρ
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Nicanor
  • Note: Son of Patroclus; general of Syrian army (1Macc 3:38; 2Macc 8:9)
  • Note: Nicanor Seleucus: Governor of Cyprus (2Macc 12:2; 4Macc 3:20)
  • Note: One of seven men chosen to oversee the provisions for widows and poor (Acts 6:5)
  • Forms:
    • Νικάνορα Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Νικάνορι Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Νικάνορος Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Νικήτη
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Nicetes
  • Note: The father of Herod the police captain (Mart-pol 8:2; 17:2)
  • Forms:
    • Νικήτην Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Νικήτης Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Νικόδημος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Nicodemus
  • Note: Pharisee and member of the Sanhedrin (John 3:1-21)
  • Forms:
    • Νικοδήμου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Νικολαΐτης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Nicolaitans
  • Note: A heretical group that practised false freedom (Rev 2:6)
  • Forms:
    • Νικολαϊτῶν Noun: Gen Plur Masc
Νικόλαος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Nicolas, Nicolaus
  • Note: One of seven men chosen to oversee the provisions for widows and poor (Acts 6:5)
  • Forms:
    • Νικόλαον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Νικολάου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Νικόπολις
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Nicopolis
  • Note: A city in Epirus built by Augustus Caesar in 31BC (Titus 3:12)
  • Forms:
    • Νικοπόλεως Noun: Gen Sing Fem
    • Νικόπολιν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
Νινευή, Νινευῆ
  • Parse: Noun: Nom/Acc/Gen Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Nineveh
  • Note: Capital of the Assyrian Empire, located in north Mesopotamia (Jonah 1:1ff)
  • Forms:
    • Νινευῇ Noun: Dat Sing Fem
Νινευήτης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Nineveh
  • Note: (Tobit 2:2)
  • Forms:

Νινευΐ
  • Parse: Noun: indeclined Fem

  • Note: (Gen 10:11)
Νινευίτης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Ninevite
  • Note: demonym of Nineveh (1Clem 7:7)
  • Forms:
    • Νινευῖται Noun: Nom Plur Masc
    • Νινευΐταις, Νινευίταις Noun: Dat Plur Masc
Νισά
  • Meaning: Nisan
  • Note: The first month in the Jewish calendar (Esther 1:1)
Νισάν
  • Meaning: Nisan
  • Note: The first month in the Jewish calendar (Esther 8:9)
Νισία, Νισιά
  • Meaning: Neziah
  • Note: Leader of some Nethanim who led captives out of Babylon (Ezra 2:54)
Νιφίς
  • Meaning: Nephis, Niphis
