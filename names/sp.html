Σπανία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Spain
  • Note: The furthest land of the western end of the Mediterranean (Rom 15:24,28)
  • Forms:
    • Σπανίαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Σπανίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Σπάρτη
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Sparta
  • Note: Prominent city state of Greece. (1Macc 14:16) Also known as Lacedaemon (2Macc 5:9)
  • Forms:
    • Σπάρτης Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Σπαρτιᾶται
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Plur Masc
  • Meaning: Spartans
  • Note: Citizens of Sparta (1Macc 12:2ff; 14:20ff; 15:23)
  • Forms:
    • Σπαρτιάταις Noun: Dat Plur Masc
    • Σπαρτιάτας Noun: Acc Plur Masc
    • Σπαρτιατῶν Noun: Gen Plur Masc
