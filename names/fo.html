Φογώρ, Φόγωρ
  • Meaning: Pai, Peor, Phogor, Pau
  • Note: Pai/Pau: City where Hadad king of Edom lived (Gen 36:39; 1Chron 1:50)
  • Note: Pau: City of Edom where Hadar reigned (Gen 36:39)
  • Note: Peor: Beth-Peor city in Moab on the east of Jordan opposite Jericho (Deut 3:29; 4:46; 34:6; Josh 13:20; 22:17)
  • Note: Phogor: Possibly Peor (Tobit 1:2)
  • Note: Peor: Mountain peak from which Balaam pronounced a curse on Israel (Num 23:28)
Φοίβη
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Phoebe
  • Note: Phoebe of Cenchrea sent the "Epistle to the Romans" to Rome (Rom 16:1)
  • Forms:
    • Φοίβην Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Φοίβης Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Φοῖνιξ
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Phoenix, Phoenician
  • Note: Phoenix: Town with a harbour on the south side of Crete, west of Fair Havens (Acts 27:12)
  • Note: A race of people who lived in the area of Tyre and Sidon (Deut 3:9)
  • Forms:
    • Φοίνιξι Noun: Dat Plur Masc
    • Φοίνικα Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Φοίνικες Noun: Nom Plur Masc
    • Φοινίκων Noun: Gen Plur Masc
Φοινίκη
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Phoenicia, Phenice
  • Note: A strip of land about 30 km wide along the shore of the Mediterranean from the Eleutherus river in the north to the promontory of Carmel in the south, about 200 km in length. (Acts 11:19; 21:2)
  • Forms:
    • Φοινίκην Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Φοινίκῃ Noun: Dat Sing Fem
    • Φοινίκης Noun: Gen Sing Fem
