Λύδδα
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Lydda
  • Note: Town in the territory of Ephraim located about 15 km east of Joppa (Acts 9:32,35,38)
  • Forms:
    • Λύδδαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Λύδδης Noun: Gen Sing Fem
    • Λύδδας Noun: Acc Plur Fem
Λυδία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Lydia
  • Note: A woman of Thyatira who lived in Philippi (Acts 16:14,15)
  • Note: An important country in the western part of Asia Minor bounded on the North by Mysia, on the East by Phrygia, on the South by Caria, and on the West by the Aegean Sea (1Macc 8:8)
  • Forms:
    • Λυδίαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Λυδίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Λυδός
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Lydians
  • Note: Natives of the province of west Asia Minor (Ezek 30:5; Jer 46:9)
  • Forms:
    • Λυδοί Noun: Nom Plur Masc
Λυκαονία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Lycaonia
  • Note: Inland province of Asia Minor west of Cappadocia and south of Galatia. (Acts 14:11)
  • Forms:
    • Λυκαονίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Λυκαονιστί
  • Parse: Adverb
  • Meaning: Lycaonian
  • Note: Lycaonian language, dialect of Lycaonia (Acts 14:11)
Λυκία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Lycia
  • Note: Province in SW of Asia Minor opposite island of Rhodes (Acts 21:1; 27:5)
  • Forms:
    • Λυκίαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Λυκίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Λυσανίας
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Lysanias
  • Note: Tetrarch of Abilene (Luke 3:1)
  • Forms:
    • Λυσανίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Λυσίας
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Lysias
  • Note: Claudius Lysias: a chief captain who intervened when the Jews tried to harm Paul (Acts 23:26)
  • Note: A nobleman of royal blood (1Macc 3:32; 2Macc 10:11)
  • Forms:
    • Λυσίᾳ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Λυσίαν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Λυσίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Λυσίμαχος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Lysimachus
  • Note: Son of Ptolemy of Jerusalem (Esther 11:1)
  • Note: Brother of Menelaus (2Macc 4:29)
  • Forms:
    • Λυσίμαχον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Λυσιμάχου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Λύστρα
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Lystra
  • Note: Town of Lycaonia in Asia Minor (Acts 14:2-7; 2Tim 3:11)
  • Forms:
    • Λύστραν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Λύστρας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
    • Λύστροις Noun: Dat Plur Fem
