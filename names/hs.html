Ἠσαίας, Ἠσαΐας, Ἡσαΐας
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Isaiah, Esaias
  • Note: Son of Amoz; prophet during the reign of Uzziah (or Azariah), Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah (Isa 1:1; 2:1)
  • Forms:
    • Ἠσαίᾳ, Ἠσαΐᾳ, Ἡσαΐᾳ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Ἠσαίαν, Ἠσαΐαν, Ἡσαΐαν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Ἠσαίου, Ἠσαΐου, Ἡσαΐου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Ἠσάμ
  • Meaning: Hashum
  • Note: One of the leaders of the people who endorsed Nehemiah's covenant (Neh 10:19)
Ἡσαμαθίμ
  • Meaning: Shumathites
  • Note: Descendants of Caleb who lived in Kiriath-Jearim (1Chron 2:53)
Ἠσαρεΐ
  • Meaning: Zorites
  • Note: Descendants of Salma (1Chron 2:54)
Ἠσαρί
  • Meaning: Zorites, Esarites
  • Note: Descendants of Salma
  • Concord: 1Chron 2:54
Ἠσαῦ, Ἡσαῦ, Ἠσαύ
  • Meaning: Esau
  • Note: Son of Isaac and Rebekah, brother of Jacob (Gen 25:25)
  • Note: Head of family of temple servants (1Esdras 5:29)
Ἠσερέ

Ἠσυηλός
