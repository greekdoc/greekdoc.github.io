Ῥήγιον
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Neut
  • Meaning: Rhegium
  • Note: Town in the south of Italy on the Strait of Messina (Acts 28:13)
Ῥηί
  • Meaning: Rei
  • Note: Rei, Shimei and the Gibborim who belonged to David are listed among those who did not join Adonijah in his attempt on the throne (1Kings 1:8)
Ῥημώθ
  • Meaning: Ramoth
  • Note: Son of Bani who promised to put away his pagan wife (Ezra 10:29)
Ῥησά
  • Meaning: Rhesa
  • Note: Son of Zerubbabel and father of Joannas (Luke 3:27)
Ῥησαίας
