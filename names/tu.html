Τύραννος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Tyrannus
  • Note: Greek rhetorician in whose school in Ephesus, Paul disputed (Acts 19:9)
  • Forms:
    • Τυράννου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Τύριος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc/Fem
  • Meaning: Tyrian, demonym of Tyre
  • Note: LXX takes to be Tyrian while others name it as Ziddim, a city in the territory of Naphtali (Josh 19:34)
  • Forms:
    • Τύριοι Noun: Nom Plur Masc
    • Τυρίοις Noun: Dat Plur Masc
    • Τυρίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
    • Τυρίων Noun: Gen Plur Masc
Τύρος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Tyre
  • Note: Phoenician city on the east coast of the Mediterranean Sea
  • Note: LXX takes to be Tyre while others name it as a city in the territory of Naphtali (Josh 19:35)
  • Forms:
    • Τύρον Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Τύρου Noun: Gen Sing Fem
    • Τύρῳ Noun: Dat Sing Fem
Τύχικος, Τυχικός
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Tychicus
  • Note: Partner with Paul and Trophimus (Eph 6:21,22)
  • Forms:
    • Τύχικον, Τυχικόν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Τυχικοῦ Noun: Gen Sing Masc
