Μύνδος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Myndos, Myndus
  • Note: City of Asia Minor on west shore (1Macc 15:23)
  • Forms:
    • Μύνδον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
Μύρα
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Plur Neut
  • Meaning: Myra
  • Note: Chief town of Lycia in Asia Minor about 4 km from the coast (Acts 27:5)
  • Forms:
    • Μύρων Noun: Gen Plur Neut
Μυρσινῶν
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Myrsinon
  • Note: Place that Vaticanus Codex LXX has in place of Aijalon (Judg 1:35)
  • Forms:
    • Μυρσινῶνι Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Μυρσινῶνος Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Μυσάρχης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Mysian
  • Note: Country in the northwestern part of Asia Minor (2Macc 5:24)
  • Forms:
    • Μυσάρχην Noun: Acc Sing Masc
Μυσία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Mysia
  • Note: Province in NW of Asia Minor (Acts 16:7,8)
  • Forms:
    • Μυσίαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Μυσίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
