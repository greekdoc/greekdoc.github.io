Ἐχά
  • Meaning: Jahath
  • Note: Son of Gershon and father of Shimei (1Chron 6:43)
Ἐχελά
  • Meaning: Hachilah
  • Note: one of the peaks of the long ridge of el-Kolah, running out of the Ziph plateau (1Sam 23:19)
Ἐχθρία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Enmity, Sitnah
  • Note: LXX translates as Ἐχθρία while others transliterate it as Sitnah (Gen 26:21)
Ἐχοζόβ
  • Meaning: Achzib
  • Note: City in the territory of Asher (Josh 19:29)
Ἐχράν
  • Meaning: Ocran
  • Note: Father of Pagiel (Num 1:13)
Ἐχωχί
  • Meaning: Ahohite
  • Note: Descendant of Ahoah (1Chron 27:4)
