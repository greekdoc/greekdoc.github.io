Τώβ
  • Meaning: Tob
  • Note: The land to which Jephthah fled, east of Jodan, SE of Sea of Galilee (Judg 11:3). Also called Ish-Tob (Ἰστώβ) (2Sam 10:6,8)
Τωβίας
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Tobit, Tobias
  • Note: Tobit: Author or hero of the book that bears his name (Tobit 1:1)
  • Note: Tobias: Son of Tobit and Anna (Tobit 1:20)
  • Note: Tobijah: A Levite whom Jehoshaphat sent out to teach (2Chron 17:8)
  • Note: Tobiah: A man who could not prove lineage to Israel (Ezra 2:60; Neh 7:62)
  • Note: Tobiah the Ammonite (Neh 2:10,19; 4:3)
  • Forms:
    • Τωβεί Noun: Voc Sing Masc
    • Τωβία Noun
    • Τωβίᾳ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Τωβίαν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Τωβίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Τωβιήλ
  • Meaning: Tobiel
  • Note: Son of Hananiel and father of Tobit (Tobit 1:1)
Τωβίθ
