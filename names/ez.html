Ἐζεκίας, Ἑζεκίας
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Hezekiah, Jehizkiah
  • Note: Jehizkiah: the son of Shallum from the tribe of Ephraim (2Chron 28:12)
  • Note: Hezekiah: Son of Ahaz and father of Manasseh (2Kings 18:1; 2Chron 29:1; Matt 1:9)
  • Note: Son of Neariah of the royal family of Judah (1Chron 3:23)
  • Note: Father of Amariah; ancestor of Zephaniah (Zeph 1:1)
  • Note: One of the returned exiles from Babylon (Ezra 2:16; Neh 7:21)
  • Forms:
    • Ἐζεκίᾳ, Ἑζεκίᾳ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Ἐζεκίαν, Ἑζεκίαν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Ἐζεκίου, Ἑζεκίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
