Ὢδ
  • Meaning: Hod
  • Note: Son of Zophah (1Chron 7:37)
Ὠδήδ, Ὡδήδ
  • Meaning: Ader, Oded
  • Note: Ader: son of Beriah (1Chron 8:15)
  • Note: Oded: the father of Azariah (2Chron 15:1)
  • Note: Oded: a prophet in Samaria (2Chron 28:9)
Ὠδιήλ
  • Meaning: Adiel
  • Note: Father of Azmaveth (1Chron 27:25)
Ὠδουέ
  • Meaning: Uri
  • Note: Gatekeeper who promised to put away his pagan wife (Ezra 10:24)
Ὠδούθ
  • Meaning: Uri
  • Note: Gatekeeper who promised to put away his pagan wife (Ezra 10:24)
Ὠδουία, Ὠδουΐα
  • Meaning: Hodaviah, Oduia
  • Note: Hodaviah: Mighty man of valour and head of his father's house (1Chron 5:24)
  • Note: Hodaviah: son of Hassenuah and father of Meshullam (1Chron 9:7)
  • Note: Hodaviah: Levite father of Jeshua and Kadmiel (Ezra 2:40)
  • Note: Hodijah: one of the Levites who endorsed Nehemiah's covenant (Neh 10:11)
  • Note: Hodijah: another one of the Levites who endorsed Nehemiah's covenant (Neh 10:14)
Ὠδούμ
  • Meaning: Hodijah
