Βύβλιοι
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Byblos, Gebal
  • Note: Phoenician city, not far from the sea coast, to the north of Beirut (Beyrout) (Ezek. 27:9); called by the Greeks Byblos
  • Forms:
