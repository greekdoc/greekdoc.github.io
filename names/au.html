  • Meaning: Ivah

Αὐαράν
  • Meaning: Avaran

  • Note: Daughter of Pharzellaius, Barzelus (1Esdras 5:38)
  • Forms:

Αὔγουστος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Caesar Augustus
  • Note: First Roman emperor; original name Caius Octavius Caepias (63BC-AD14) (Luke 2:1)
  • Forms:
    • Αὐγούστου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Αὐδῶν
  • Parse: Noun:

Αὐίμ
  • Meaning: Ijim
  • Note: City in the territory of Judah (Josh 15:29)
Αὐλών
  • Meaning: Aulon

Αὒν
  • Meaning: On
  • Note: Son of Peleth (Num 16:1)
Αὐνάν
  • Meaning: Onan
  • Note: Aner: A Canaanite chief who joined his forces with those of Abraham in pursuit of Chedorlaomer (Gen. 14:13,24)
  • Note: Onan: Son of Judah and Shua (Gen 38:4-10; 46:12)
