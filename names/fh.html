Φῆστος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Festus
  • Note: Porcius Festus, successor of Felix as procurator of Judea (Acts 24:27)
  • Forms:
    • Φῆστε Noun: Voc Sing Masc
    • Φῆστον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Φῆστου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
    • Φῆστῳ Noun: Gen Sing Masc
