Ζιζά
  • Meaning: Ziza
  • Note: Ziza: son of Rehoboam and Maacah (2Chron 11:20)
  • Note: Ziza: Another Ziza (spelled Ζουζά in the LXX) was a family leader in the tribe of Simeon, the son of Shiphi (1Chron 4:37)
  • Note: Zina: son of Shimei (1Chron 23:10)
  • Note: Zizah son of Shimei (1Chron 23:11)

Ζίφ
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Ziph
  • Note: Grandson of Caleb (1Chron 2:42)
  • Note: A city in the mountains of Judah where David hid himself (1Sam 23:14,19), called Wilderness of Ziph. Also mentioned in Josh 15:55
  • Note: A city in the south of Judah (Josh 15:24, but not in LXX)
  • Note: One of the cities that Rehoboam built for defence (2Chron 11:8)
  • Note: Son of Mesha (1Chron 2:42)
Ζιφαῖος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Ziphites
  • Note: demonyms of Ziph where David was hiding (1Sam 23:19; 26:1; Ps 53 title)
  • Forms:
