Πρίσκα
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Prisca
  • Note: Wife of Aquila, also called Priscilla (Rom 16:3)
  • Forms:
    • Πρίσκαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Πρίσκης Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Πρίσκιλλα

Προμηθεύς
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Prometheus (son of the Titan Iapetus and Themis, inventor of many arts: he is said to have made man from clay)
  • Forms:
    • Προμηθέα Noun: Acc Sing Masc
Πρόχορος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Prochorus
  • Note: One of seven men chosen to oversee the provisions for widows and poor (Acts 6:5)
  • Forms:
    • Πρόχορον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Πρόχορου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
