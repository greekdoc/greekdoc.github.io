Μηδαβά
  • Meaning: Medeba
  • Note: City located about 10 km south of Heshbon (Num 21:30; 1Macc 9:36)
Μηδία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Media
  • Note: Land of the Medes located in what is now NW Iran (1Macc 8:8; Tobit 14:4)
  • Forms:
    • Μηδίᾳ Noun: Dat Sing Fem
    • Μηδίαν Noun: Gen Sing Fem
    • Μηδίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Μηδική
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Medices
  • Note: Medes (Dan 9:1)
  • Forms:
    • Μηδικῆς Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Μῆδος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Mede, native of Media in Asia Minor
  • Forms:
    • Μῆδοι Noun: Nom Plur Masc
    • Μήδοις Noun: Dat Plur Masc
    • Μήδους Noun: Acc Plur Masc
    • Μήδων Noun: Gen Plur Masc
    • Μήδου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Μηρώζ
  • Meaning: Meroz
  • Note: Neh 3:7
Μηρωνωθίτης
  • Meaning: Meronothite
  • Note: a plain in the north of Palestine (Judg 5:23 Vat)
