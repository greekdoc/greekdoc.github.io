Ἠμαδαβούν
  • Meaning: Madiabun, Emadaboun
  • Note: Head of family who worked on temple (1Esdras 5:56)
Ἠμάθ, Ἡμάθ
  • Meaning: Hamath, Hethlon, Hemath
  • Note: Royal city of the Hittites (Jer 49:23; 1Chron 13:5)
Ἡμασαραΐμ
  • Meaning: Mishraites
  • Note: Descendants of Caleb who lived in Kiriath Jearim (1Chron 2:53)
Ἡμεΐ
  • Meaning: Ami
  • Note: Servants of Solomon who came out of Babylon with Zerubbabel (Ezra 2:57)
Ἡμέραν
  • Meaning: Emeran, Jemimah
  • Note: Job's first daughter (Job 42:14)
Ἠμί
  • Meaning: Ami
  • Note: Servants of Solomon who came out of Babylon with Zerubbabel (Ezra 2:57)
Ἠμίμ
  • Meaning: Amon
  • Note: Family of Solomon's servants who returned from Babylonian captivity (Neh 7:59)
Ἡμισουσεωσίν
  • Meaning: Hazarsusim
  • Note: Cities where the descendants of Shimei dwelt
  • Concord: 1Chron 4:31
