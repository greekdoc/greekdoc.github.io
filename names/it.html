Ἰταβύριον
  • Meaning: Tabor, Itabyrium
  • Note: Town in the territory of Zebulun, west of the Jordan and south of the Sea of Galilee (Jer 46:18)
Ἰταλία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Italy
  • Note: The peninsular country west of Greece (Acts 18:2; 27:1,6; Heb 13:24)
  • Forms:
    • Ἰταλίαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Ἰταλίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Ἰταλικός
  • Parse: Adj: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Italian
  • Note: Roman cohort in which Cornelius belonged, so named because the men were recruited in Italy (Acts 10:1)
  • Forms:
    • Ἰταλικῆς Adj: Gen Sing Fem
Ἰτάν
  • Meaning: Juttah
  • Note: City in the hill country of the territory of Judah (Josh 15:55)
Ἰτουραῖος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Iturean, Jetur
  • Note: Arabian nomads (1Chron 5:19)
  • Forms:
    • Ἰτουραίων Noun: Gen Plur Masc
Ἰτουραία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Iturea
  • Note: District in NE Palestine (Luke 3:1)
  • Forms:
    • Ἰτουραίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
