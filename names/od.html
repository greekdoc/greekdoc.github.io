  • Meaning: Hod
  • Note: Son of Zophah (1Chron 7:37)
Ὀδιήλ
  • Meaning: Adiel
  • Note: Father of Azmaveth
  • Concord: 1Chron 27:25
Ὀδολία
  • Meaning: Hodaiah
  • Note: Son of Elioenai
  • Concord: 1Chron 3:24
Ὀδολλάμ
  • Meaning: Adullam
  • Note: Adullam: City in the territory of Judah (Josh 15:35)
  • Note: Eglon: City where Debir was king (Josh 10:3)
Ὀδολλαμίτης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Adullamite
  • Note: A native of the city of Adullam (Gen 38:1,12,20)
  • Forms:
    • Ὀδολλαμίτην Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Ὀδολλαμίτου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Ὀδομηρά
  • Meaning: Odomera
  • Note: It is not certain whether Odomera was an independent Bedouin chief, perhaps an ally of the Syrians, or an officer of Bacchides. He was defeated by Jonathan in his campaign against Bacchides (1 Macc 9:66) in 156 BC.
Ὀδόμ
  • Meaning: Eden
  • Note: Levite under Kore (2Chron 31:14)
Ὁδορρά
  • Meaning: Hadoram
  • Note: Son of Joktan (Gen 10:27)
Ὁδουία
  • Meaning: Hodijah, Hodaviah
  • Note: Hodijah: One of the leaders of the people who endorsed Nehemiah's covenant (Neh 10:19)
  • Note: Hodaviah: Son of Elioenai (1Chron 3:24)
Ὀδουιάς, Ὀδουΐας
  • Meaning: Ira
  • Note: Son of Ikkesh. Commander-in-chief of David's army for the sixth month (1Chron 27:9)
