Τολβανής
  • Meaning: Telem
  • Note: One of the doorkeepers who had married a foreign wife (1Esdras 9:25)
Τολμάν
  • Meaning: Talmon, Tolman
  • Note: Gatekeeper (1Esdras 5:28)
Τομμάν
  • Meaning: Tomman
  • Note: City in territory of Issachar (Josh 19:21 LXX)
Τουβάν
  • Meaning: Tobiah
  • Note: Head of family who came out of captivity but could not reveal heritage (1Esdras 5:37)
Τουβιανοί
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Toubiani
  • Note: The name of Jews in Charax (2Macc 12:17)
  • Forms:
    • Τουβιανούς Noun: Acc Plur Masc
Τουβίας
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Tob
  • Note: Land east of Jodan, SE of Sea of Galilee (1Macc 5:13)
  • Forms:
    • Τουβίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Τοφόλ
  • Meaning: Tophel
  • Note: A place in the Sinai wilderness on west side of the mountains of Edom (Deut 1:1)
Τοχός
  • Meaning: Thahash
  • Note: Son of Nahor and Reumah (Gen 22:24)
