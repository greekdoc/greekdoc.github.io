Πιθώμ
  • Meaning: Pithom
  • Note: Egyptian treasure city (Ex 1:11)
Πικρά
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Pikra, bitter
  • Forms:
    • Πικράν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
Πικρία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Marah
  • Note: Camp place after Hahiroth (Num 33:8)
  • Note: Marah is Hebrew for bitter; likewise πικρία is Greek for bitter
  • Forms:
    • Πικριῶν Noun: Gen Plur Fem
    • Πικρίαις Noun: Dat Plur Fem
Πιλάτος, Πιλᾶτος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Pilate
  • Note: Pontius Pilate: Procurator of Judea from AD26-36 (Matt 27:2; Ign-Magn 11:1; Ign-Smyrn 1:2; Ign-Trall 9:1)
  • Forms:
    • Πιλάτου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
    • Πιλάτον, Πιλᾶτον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Πιλάτῳ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
Πιόνιος
  • Meaning: Pionius
  • Note: The man who re-wrote the account of the martyrdom of Polycarp (Mart-Pol 22:3)
Πισιδία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Pisidia
  • Note: District in Asia Minor north of Pamphylia (Acts 13:14; 14:21-24)
  • Forms:
    • Πισιδίαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Πισιδίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
