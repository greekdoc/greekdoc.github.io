Γώγ
  • Meaning: Gog
  • Note: Reubenite, father of Shimei (1Chron 5:4)
  • Note: leader of the hostile party from the north (Ezek 38-39) and in the vision of Revelation (Rev 20:8)
Γωγαίου
  • Meaning: Gogaius
Γωζάν, Γωζᾶν
  • Meaning: Gozan
  • Note: Region in Central Asia to which the Israelites were carried away captive (Isa 37:1)
Γωθί
  • Meaning: Uthai
  • Note: Son of Ammihud (1Chron 9:4)
Γωίμ, Γωΐμ
  • Meaning: Goim
  • Note: Hebrew for nations (Josh 12:23)
Γωλά
  • Meaning: Giloh, Alvah
  • Note: Alvah: One of Esau's chiefs (Gen 36:40)
  • Note: Aliah: Chief of Esau (1Chron 1:51)
  • Note: LXX understands to be the name of a city, but it means and he/it ascends, goes up(Josh 19:11)
  • Note: Giloh: City of Ahithophel located in the hill country of Judah (2Sam 15:12)

Γωλάμ

  • Meaning: Alvan
  • Note: son of Shobal (Gen 36:23)
Γωλάν
  • Meaning: Golan
  • Note: city in the territory allotted to Manasseh in Bashan (1Chron 6:71)
Γωλών
  • Meaning: Alvan
  • Note: Son of Shobal (Gen 36:23)
Γωνάθ
  • Meaning: Ginath
  • Note: Father of Tibni (1Kings 16:21)
Γωνί

  • Note: Son of Naphtali (1Chron 7:13)
Γωϋνί, Γωυνί
  • Meaning: Gouni, Guni
