Ἀαδαί
  • Meaning: Ahlai, Aadai
  • Note: Ahlai: Daughter of Sheshan; because she had no children, he gave her in marriage to his Egyptian slave Jarha. Her descendants were Zabad, one of David's mighty men (1Chron 11:41) and Azariah (one of the captains of hundreds under the reign of Jehoiada (2Chron 23:1).
  • Note: Also spelled Ἀχλαΐ
Ἀαλάφ
  • Meaning: Ahlab
  • Note: a city of Asher from which the Canaanites were not driven out. (Judg 1:31)
Ἀαρά
  • Meaning: Aharah
  • Note: The third son of Benjamin (1Chron 8:1)
Ἀαρών
  • Meaning: Aaron
  • Hebrew:
  • Note: Eldest son of Amram and Jochebed; brother of Moses and Miriam
