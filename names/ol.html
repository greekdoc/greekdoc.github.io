Ὄλαμος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Olamus, Elam
  • Note: Mani's descendant who had married a foreign wife (1Esdras 9:30)
  • Note: Ancestor of 1254 people who were led out of captivity (1Esdras 5:12)
  • Forms:
    • Ὠλάμου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Ὀλδάν, Ὄλδαν
  • Meaning: Huldah
  • Note: Prophetess wife of Shallum (2Kings 22:14)
Ὀλιβεμά
  • Meaning: Aholibamah
  • Note: Daughter of Anah (Gen 36:2)
  • Note: One of Esau's chiefs (Gen 36:41)
Ὀλιβεμᾶς, Ὀλιβεμάς
  • Meaning: Aholibamah
  • Note: Daughter of Anah (Gen 36:2)
  • Note: One of Esau's chiefs (Gen 36:41)
Ὀλοφέρνης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Holofernes
  • Note: An invading general of Nebuchadnezzar who was beheaded by Judith (Judith ; 1Clem 55:5)
  • Forms:
    • Ὀλοφέρνη Noun: Voc Sing Masc
    • Ὀλοφέρνῃ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Ὀλοφέρνην Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Ὀλοφέρνου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Ὀλυμπᾶς
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Olympas
  • Note: A Christian in the church in Rome to whom Paul sends greetings (Rom 16:15)
  • Forms:
    • Ὀλυμπᾶν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Ὀλυμπᾶ Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Ὀλυμπίου
  • Meaning: Olympius, Olympian
  • Note: An epithet of JUPITER or ZEUS (which see) from Mt. Olympus in Thessaly, where the gods held court presided over by Zeus. Antiochus Epiphanes, "who on God's altars danced," insulted the Jewish religion by dedicating the temple of Jerusalem to Jupiter Olympius, 168 BC (2Macc 6:2)
