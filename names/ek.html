Ἑκαταῖος
  • Parse: Name
  • Meaning: Hecataios
Ἐκβάτανα
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Ecbatane, a city of the Medes
  • Forms:
    • Ἐκβατάνοις Noun: Dat Plur Masc
    • Ἐκβατάνων Noun: Gen Plur Masc
Ἐκθαναάδ
  • Meaning: Ecthanaad
  • Note: City that Manasseh did not drive out, possibly Taanach (Judg 1:27)
Ἐκκάς
  • Meaning: Ikkesh
  • Note: Father of Ira (2Sam 23:26)
Ἐκκής, Ἐκκῆς
  • Meaning: Ikkesh
  • Note: Father of Ira (1Chron 11:28)
Ἐκκλησιαστής
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Ecclesiastes
  • Note: Greek rendering of Hebrew Koheleth meaning Preacher (Eccl 1:1)
  • Forms:
    • Ἐκκλησιαστοῦ Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Ἐκκίς
  • Meaning: Ikkesh
  • Note: Father of Ira (1Chron 11:28)
Ἐκχεχάρ
  • Meaning: Ekchechar
  • Note: Transliteration of which means the plain (Neh 3:22). It also means circle and thus may refer to the lower Jordan valley
