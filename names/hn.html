Ἠναδάδ
  • Meaning: Henadad
  • Note: Father of Binnui, one of the Levites who endorsed Nehemiah's covenant (Ezra 3:9; Neh 3:24; 10:10)
  • Note: Father of Bavai (Neh 3:18)
Ἠναδδά
  • Meaning: En Haddah
  • Note: Town in territory of Issachar (Josh 19:21)
Ἠναῒμ
  • Meaning: Enam
  • Note: City in the territory of Judah (Josh 15:34)
Ἠνάμ
  • Meaning: Anan
  • Note: One of the leaders of the people who endorsed Nehemiah's covenant (Neh 10:27)
Ἠνάν
  • Meaning: Anan
  • Note: One of the leaders of the people who endorsed Nehemiah's covenant (Neh 10:27)
Ἠνγαδδί
  • Meaning: En Gedi
  • Note: Place in the wilderness of Judah on the west shore of Dead Sea (Josh 15:61)
Ἠνγαννίμ
  • Meaning: En Gannim
  • Note: Town in the territory of Issachar (Josh 19:21)
Ἡνεία
  • Meaning: Hēneia
  • Note: Name mentioned only in LXX (1Chron 25:9)
Ἠνία
  • Meaning: Enia
  • Note: Name mentioned only in LXX (1Chron 25:9)
