Σκευᾶς
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Sceva
  • Note: A chief priest who had 7 sons who tried to exorcise demons (Acts 19:14)
  • Forms:
    • Σκευᾶ Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Σκύθης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Scythian
  • Note: A native of Scythia north of the Black Sea and Caspian Sea (Col 3:11; 2Macc 4:47; 12:29; 3Macc 7:5; Judith 3:10; Judg 1:27)
  • Note: In Judith 3:10 Σκυθῶν πόλεως may be considered as one word: Scythopolis
  • Forms:
    • Σκύθου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
    • Σκυθῶν Noun: Gen Plur Masc
Σκυθοπολῖται
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Plur Masc
  • Meaning: Scythopolitans
  • Note: Scythian cities (2Macc 12:30)
