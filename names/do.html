Δομετιανός
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Domitian
  • Note: Roman emperor, AD51-96 when he was assassinated; succeeded by Nerva (Pap 6:1)
  • Forms:
    • Δομετιανόν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
Δορκάς
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Dorcas
  • Note: Pious widow at Joppa whom Peter restored to life (Acts 9:36-41)
  • Forms:
    • Δορκάδος Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Δορκών
  • Meaning: Darkon
  • Note: Family of Solomon's servants who returned from Babylonian captivity (Neh 7:58)
Δορυμένης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Dorymenes
  • Note: Father of Ptolemy Macron who fought against Antiochus the Great (1Macc 3:38; 2Macc 4:45)
  • Forms:
    • Δορυμένους Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Δοσίθεος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Dositheus
  • Note: Captain of Judas Maccabeus (2Macc 12:19ff)
  • Note: Soldier in army of Judas Maccabeus (2Macc 12:35)
  • Note: Jew, son of Drimylus who rescued Ptolemy Philopator (3Macc 1:3)
  • Note: Levite priest who "in the 4th year of the reign of Ptolemy and Cleopatra" carried the translation of the Book of Esther to Alexandria (Esther 11:1)
  • Forms:
    • Δοσίθεον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
