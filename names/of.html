Ὀφάζ
  • Meaning: Ophaz
  • Note: Place where there is gold (Dan 10:5)
Ὀφάρ
  • Meaning: Hepher
  • Note: One of David's mighty warriors (1Chron 11:36)
Ὀφέρ, Ὄφερ
  • Meaning: Opher, Hepher, Epher
  • Note: Epher: Son of Midian (Gen 25:3)
  • Note: Hepher: Gileadite father of Zelophehad (Num 26:32)
  • Note: Hepher: Canaanitish town mentioned between Tappuah and Aphek (Josh 12:17)
  • Note: Epher: Leader of tribe of Manasseh (1Chron 5:24)
Ὀφερί
  • Meaning: Hepherite
  • Note: Descendant of Hepher (Num 26:32)
Ὁφιμίν, Ὀφιμίν
  • Meaning: Huppim
  • Note: Son of Benjamin (Gen 46:21)
Ὀφλά
  • Meaning: Ophel
  • Note: A section of the temple wall (Neh 3:27)
Ὀφνί
  • Meaning: Ophni
  • Note: Son of Eli (1Sam 1:3)
