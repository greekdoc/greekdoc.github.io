Ἐθέρ
  • Meaning: Ether
  • Note: City in the territory of the tribe of Simeon (Josh 19:7)
Ἐθθεναῖος
  • Meaning: Ithrite
  • Note: Demonym of Gareb, one of David's 30 men
  • Concord: 2Sam 23:38
Ἐθθί
  • Meaning: Ittai, Attai, Eththi
  • Note: Ittai: man of Gath (2Sam 15:19)
  • Note: Ittai: son of Ribai; one of David's 30 mighty men (2Sam 23:29)
  • Note: Attai: Son of Jarha (1Chron 2:35)
  • Note: Attai: Gadite man in David's army (1Chron 12:11)
Ἐθί
  • Meaning: Hittites
  • Note: (Ezra 9:1)
Ἐθιήλ
  • Meaning: Ithiel
  • Note: Son of Jeshaiah and father of Maaseiah
  • Concord: Neh 11:7
Ἐθνάν
  • Meaning: Ethnan
  • Note: Son of Ashhur (1Chron 4:7)
