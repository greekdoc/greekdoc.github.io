Ὀρέχ
  • Meaning: Erech
  • Note: Erech: Kingdom of the east established by Nimrod (Gen 10:10)
  • Note: Arah: Son of Ulla (1Chron 7:39)
Ὀρή
  • Meaning: Ore
  • Note: LXX reads as proper noun of a city, but it means cities of (2Kings 17:6; 18:11)
Ὀρήρ
  • Meaning: Arad
  • Note: Son of Beriah (1Chron 8:15)
Ὀρθωσία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Orthosia
  • Note: City to which Tryphon fled when he escaped from Dora, where he was besieged by Antiochus Sidetes (1Macc 15:37)
  • Forms:
    • Ὀρθωσίαν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
Ὀρίωνος
  • Meaning: Orion
  • Note: Constellation of about 80 visible stars (Job 38:31)
Ὀρνά
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Orna, Arnan
  • Note: Araunah: Owner of a threshing floor (2Sam 24:16)
  • Note: Ardon: Son of Caleb and Azubah (1Chron 2:18)
  • Note: Arnan: Descendants of David (1Chron 3:21)
  • Note: Ornan: Jebusite who owned a threshing floor (1Chron 21:15)
  • Forms:
    • Ὀρνάν Noun: Acc Sing Masc
Ὀρνία
  • Meaning: Adonijah
  • Note: Son of David and Haggith (2Sam 3:4)
Ὀρνίας
  • Parse: Name
  • Meaning: Ornias
Ὀρνίου
  • Meaning: Orniou
  • Note: Son of Nathan (1Kings 2:46h). Possibly Azariah or Zabud (1Kings 4:5)
Ὀρφά
  • Meaning: Orpah
  • Note: Daughter-in-law of Naomi; wife of Chilion (Ruth 1:4)
