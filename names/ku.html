Κυαμωνός
  • Meaning: Cyamon, Cynamon
  • Note: A place near Esdrelon (i.e., Jezreel) (Judith 7:3)
Κυδιώς
  • Meaning: Kedesh
  • Note: A city of refuge in the territory of the tribe of Naphtali (Tobit 1:2)
Κυπριάρχης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Cyprus
  • Note: Large island of the Mediterranean about 100 km from the Syrian coast. Called "Chittim" in the OT (Num 24:24)
Κύπριος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Cypriot
  • Note: demonym of Cyprus (Acts 4:36)
  • Forms:
    • Κύπριοι Noun: Nom Plur Masc
    • Κυπρίῳ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Κυπρίων Noun: Gen Plur Masc
Κύπρος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Cyprus
  • Note: Large island of the Mediterranean about 100 km from the Syrian coast. Called "Chittim" in the OT (Num 24:24)
  • Forms:
    • Κύπρον Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Κύπρου Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Κυρηναῖοι
  • Meaning: Cyrene
  • Note: City in Upper Libya, North Africa. Now Tripoli. (Acts 11:20)
Κυρηναῖος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Cyrenian
  • Note: demonym of Cyrene (Acts 6:9)
  • Forms:
    • Κυρηναίων Noun: Gen Plur Masc
    • Κυρηναῖον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Κυρηναίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Κυρήνη
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Cyrene
  • Note: One of the five cities in Libya; now called Tripoli (Acts 2:10)
  • Forms:
    • Κυρήνην Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Κυρήνης Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Κυρήνιος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Quirinius, Cyrenius
  • Note: Publius Sulpicius Quirinus: governor of Cilicia which was annexed to Syria (Luke 2:2)
  • Forms:
    • Κυρηνίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Κύριλλος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Cyril of Jerusalem (c. AD 313-386)
  • Forms:
    • Κυρίλλου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Κῦρος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Cyrus
  • Note: King of Persia (Elam) who conquered Babylon; son of Cambyses; born BC599 (Ezra 1:1; 1Esdras 2:1)
  • Forms:
    • Κύρου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
    • Κύρῳ Noun: Dat Sing Masc
