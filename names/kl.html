Κλαύδη
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Clauda
  • Note: A small island near Crete, also spelled Καῦδα (Acts 27:16)
  • Forms:
    • Κλαύδην Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Κλαύδης Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Κλαυδία
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Claudia
  • Note: Member in church in Rome who remained faithful to Paul (2Tim 4:21)
  • Forms:
    • Κλαυδίας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Κλαύδιος
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Claudius
  • Note: Surnamed Ephebus, messenger of Clement (1Clem 65:1)
  • Note: Fourth Roman emperor who succeeded Caligula in AD41 (Acts 18:2)
  • Note: Claudius Lysias: a Greek who purchased Roman citizenship (Acts 21:31-40; 22:28; 23:26)
  • Forms:
    • Κλαύδιον Noun: Acc Sing Masc
    • Κλαυδίου Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Κλεοπᾶς, Κλεόπας
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Cleopas
  • Note: One of two disciples whom Jesus met on the road to Emmaus (Luke 24:18)
Κλεοπάτρα
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Fem
  • Meaning: Cleopatra
  • Note: A daughter of Ptolemy VI (Philometor) and of Queen Cleopatra, who was married first to Alexander Balas 150 BC (1Macc 10:58)
  • Forms:
    • Κλεοπάτραν Noun: Acc Sing Fem
    • Κλεοπάτρας Noun: Gen Sing Fem
Κλήμης
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Clement
  • Note: Christian in church at Philippi (Phil 4:3)
  • Note: Clement of Rome (died AD99)
  • Forms:
    • Κλήμεντι Noun: Dat Sing Masc
    • Κλήμεντος Noun: Gen Sing Masc
Κλωπᾶς
  • Parse: Noun: Nom Sing Masc
  • Meaning: Clopas
  • Note: Contraction for Κλεόπας
  • Note: Husband of Mary the sister of the Virgin Mary (John 19:25)
  • Forms:
    • Κλωπᾶ Noun: Gen Sing Masc
